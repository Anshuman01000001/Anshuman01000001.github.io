Welcome, this is my personal website!
I'm a high school student who fell in love with programing at the age of 13. It's astonishing what you can do when you learn to code; it's like taking the red pill! Programming peeled back a layer of reality I wasn't even aware existed, dependent so much on technology; being able to understand and be a part of something so revolutionary is truly exciting. I honestly don't know what I'd be doing if I didn't know how to program; I now know what people mean when they say ‘computer programing is an incredibly empowering skill to learn.’
